## Exam Tips Summary

1. **Use imperative commands** - Faster than writing YAML
2. **Use `--dry-run=client -o yaml`** - Generate templates
3. **Know vim basics** - Essential for editing
4. **Use kubectl explain** - Quick field reference
5. **Practice multi-container pods** - Sidecar, init patterns
6. **Master ConfigMaps/Secrets** - High-frequency topic
7. **Know probes** - Liveness, readiness, startup
8. **Helm basics** - install, upgrade, list
9. **Check namespace** - Every question
10. **Skip and return** - Don't get stuck
